Date: 2026-05-31

Status: `DRAFT_NOT_APPROVED`

This guide is for profile preparation only. Do not create public posts or start promotion before Stage 6H and Stage 6I gates are cleared.

## 1. Naming Rules

Good account names should:

- Make Palmmi recognizable.
- Emphasize personality cards, palm texture, or entertainment.
- Avoid fortune-telling, medical, relationship, money, career, and destiny claims.
- Stay usable across Xiaohongshu, Douyin, X, and WeChat.

Avoid names containing:

- 手相
- 算命
- 命运
- 财运
- 姻缘
- 医学
- 诊断
- 预测

## 3. Avatar Direction

Recommended avatar:

- A clean palm-line mark inside a simple card shape.
- High contrast at small size.
- No mystical symbols.
- No medical cross, fortune-telling icons, coins, rings, or zodiac-like claims.
- Should still look clear at 64 x 64 px.

Do not use:

- Real user palm photos.
- Dense palmistry diagrams.
- Symbols implying fate, prophecy, wealth, marriage, or diagnosis.
- QR code as avatar.
